~About Us~

National Management of Diseases Control(NMDC) is a organization dedicated to educating the public and healthcare professionals about the symptoms, burden, causes, prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of infectious diseases across the lifespan.

Vision:

Healthier lives through effective prevention and treatment of infectious diseases

Mission:

Educate the public and healthcare professionals about the symptoms, burden, causes, prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of infectious diseases across the lifespan
